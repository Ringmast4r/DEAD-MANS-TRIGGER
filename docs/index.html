üìñ What is a Dead Man's Trigger?

A dead man's trigger (or dead man's switch) is an automated system that activates when you fail to check in regularly. It's like a countdown timer that resets when you prove you're alive.

If you don't check in before the timer expires, the system automatically sends emails with your secret message, files, and important information to designated recipients.
